# EcoSort AI
### Intelligent Waste Segregation & Sustainability Assistant

## Overview

EcoSort AI is an AI-driven web application designed to address the growing challenge of improper waste disposal and low recycling awareness. The platform utilizes Artificial Intelligence and Computer Vision to identify waste materials, classify them into appropriate categories, and provide users with environmentally responsible disposal recommendations.

By encouraging proper waste segregation and recycling practices, EcoSort AI contributes towards building cleaner communities and advancing sustainable development.

---

## Problem Statement

Improper waste segregation remains one of the major environmental challenges worldwide. Recyclable and hazardous materials are often disposed of incorrectly, leading to increased landfill waste, environmental pollution, and inefficient recycling processes.

EcoSort AI aims to solve this problem by enabling users to quickly identify waste items and receive accurate disposal guidance through an AI-powered system.
